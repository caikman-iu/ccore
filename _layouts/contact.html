How can you help make CCore better?

How can you help make CCore better? Content can be one of the most valuable business assets of an organization. Trustworthy content is at the core of delivering a high quality user experience through digital communications. Thus, we’re introducing Content Core (CCore) — an extensible content management platform built to provide a source of accurate information.
